I am currently an engineer, researcher and manager at Google. Previously, I was a tenured professor of Computer Science at Vanderbilt University. My work and interests focus on

  • GenAI task-specific customization (tuning, distillation, evaluation) and LLM agents
  • Reinforcement learning and stochastic optimization for planning and decision-making under uncertainty
  • LLM reasoning and planning
